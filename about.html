Our Story

Citizens for Animal Protection is a non-prot organization, founded in 1972, which shelters, rescues and places homeless animals for adoption with loving families. CAP advocates respect and compassion for animals. CAP speaks for those who cannot speak for themselves through the following programs and services. Shelter & Care; Pet Adoptions; Spraying & Neutering; Pet Care Counseling; Lost & Found Pets; Feral Cat Assitance Program; Pets for Seniors; Humane Education; and Pet Supply Shop and CAP eBay Store.

Mission

CAP is comitter to sheltering, rescuing and placing homeless animals in loving homes. CAP advocates respect and compassion for all animal life- we speak for those who cannot speak for themselves. CAP also provides humane education to prevent animal cruelty and raise awareness in the community of the needs of animals. To full our mission. CAP has developed and established many successfull programs and services to enhance lives.
